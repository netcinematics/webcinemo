~ Orby Learns to Fly ~
Orby practices surfing on his mothers ironing board in the garage. He falls and bumps his head, and the metal board crashes into a stack of microwave ovens his dad used for melting elements. Technicolor stars shoot out! Blue and gold electro-chemical-bursts through broken glass! The ironing board is ionized, and gold positronz stream in, as it glows with a hum! The electric plug explodes! With quick thinking, Orby punches the "Cancel" button. And the positively ionized ironing board drops to a charred smoldering lump. Weeks later, Orby is looking for a science-fair project. He makes a space-kite because he wants to fly like his dad, who flies in space. Orby sneaks into the garage late at night. In the dark, he summons the courage to press the "quick cook button". The microwave springs to life. But without the golden positronz as before, the SpaceBoard hovers for the first time!
Free of negatronz, the explosion reversed the microwave-field and will only push out positronz! Which is how the SpaceBoard floats. With different energem SpaceDust (positronz, negatronz) - the board rides differently. Faster or higher with different colors and particle waves.
DO NOT TRY THIS AT HOME. IT WONT WORK. AND ALSO BECAUSE... like the ironing board - Orby is made of aminium. Which the inverted microwave only works to lighten the weight of aminium, not a hooman!
Orby takes one step onto the SpaceBoard. His foot touches, and in a glow of positronz - he is slowly ionized! Orby might be electrocuted! But, he scans his clampy and glowing robot hands. He is ok! So, with his second flat foot, he steps up onto the SpaceBoard! He opens the garage with a green laser. Then slowly down the driveway he hovers. Until he loses his balance and crashes into the garbage cans.
